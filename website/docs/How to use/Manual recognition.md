# Manual Area Recognition

Manual recognition allows you to pinpoint a specific QR code when multiple codes are present on your screen.

### How to trigger
1. Click the **QR Pick** icon in the Menu Bar and select **Scan QR Code**.
2. **OR** use the global hotkey: `Command (âŒ˜) + Shift + R`.

### The Process
- Your cursor will turn into a crosshair.
- Click and drag to draw a box around the QR or Barcode.
- Once you release the mouse, the result window will pop up automatically.

### Result Actions
Depending on the content, you can:
- **URL**: Click "Open in Browser" (the window will auto-close).
- **WiFi**: Click "Copy Password".
- **Email**: Click "Open Mail Client".
