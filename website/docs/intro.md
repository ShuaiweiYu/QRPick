# Introduction to QR Pick

**QR Pick** is a minimalist, high-performance macOS utility designed to bridge the gap between your screen and your data. Whether it's a WiFi password in a presentation, a URL in a video, or an email address on a flyer, QR Pick "picks" the information instantly.

## Why QR Pick?

- **Snappy Performance**: Built natively for macOS to ensure near-instant recognition.
- **Privacy First**: 100% offline processing. What happens on your screen stays on your Mac.
- **Smart Actions**: Don't just scan; take action. Open links, compose emails, or copy WiFi passwords with one click.

## Core Features

- **Area Capture**: Select exactly what you want to scan.
- **Full-Screen Quick Scan**: (Coming soon/Available) Scan the entire screen for codes.
- **Smart Parsing**: Support for WiFi, Mailto, URL, and Plain Text.
- **Global Hotkey**: Trigger the scanner anywhere with `Cmd + Shift + R`.
